lasagna Recipe


Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that typically consists of layers of pasta, cheese, and meat or vegetables. To make lasagna, you will need lasagna noodles, tomato sauce, ground beef, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, parmesan cheese, garlic, onion, and seasonings. To start, cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions and set them aside. Next, brown the ground beef in a pan with diced onion and garlic, and then add the tomato sauce and seasonings. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and parmesan cheese. To assemble the lasagna, spread a layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish, followed by a layer of noodles, a layer of the cheese mixture, and then repeat the layers. Finally, bake the lasagna in the oven until it is bubbly and golden brown. Serve hot and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 oz each) tomato paste
  • 2 cans (15 oz each) tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

instructions

  • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic until the meat is browned and the onion is tender.
  • Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, sugar, salt, pepper, and 2 tablespoons of the parsley to the skillet. Simmer for 1-2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  • Cook the lasagna noodles according to package directions, and drain.
  • In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, remaining parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  • To assemble the lasagna, spread 1 1/2 cups of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange a single layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread one half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles, and top with one third of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon another 1 1/2 cups of the meat sauce over the cheese, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese. Repeat the layers, ending with a final layer of meat sauce and Parmesan cheese.
  • Cover the dish with aluminum foil, and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil, and bake for an additional 25 minutes. Let the lasagna cool for 15 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
